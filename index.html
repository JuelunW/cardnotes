5. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a resident of the European Union, you maintain comprehensive legal control over your personal infrastructure. Since we do not possess access to your local on-device files, you can exercise your statutory rights directly:

  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Handled seamlessly by you through uninstalling the app or wiping app data in your device settings.
  • Right to Complain: You have the legal right to file an official complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (Tietosuojavaltuutettu) via tietosuoja.fi if you have concerns regarding software distribution practices.
